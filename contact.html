Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our drone services

How quickly can you respond to a service request?
We typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent inspections, we can often mobilize within 24-48 hours depending on location and weather conditions.
Are your pilots licensed and insured?
Yes! All our pilots are FAA Part 107 certified and carry $2 million in liability insurance. We provide certificates of insurance upon request for any project.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve the greater Los Angeles area, but we have pilots available for projects nationwide. Contact us for remote locations—we can make arrangements!
What happens if weather cancels my flight?
Safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unsafe for flight, we'll work with you to reschedule at no additional cost. We monitor forecasts closely and communicate proactively.
Do you offer custom drone training?
Absolutely! We offer one-on-one and group training sessions tailored to your needs—from basic flight skills to advanced commercial operations and FAA test preparation.
