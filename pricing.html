Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What's included in the monthly plans?
All monthly plans include flight time, professional pilot services, post-processing, and delivery. Flight hours reset monthly and unused hours do not roll over.
Do you offer one-time project pricing?
Yes! We offer custom quotes for one-time projects. Contact our sales team with your requirements for a free estimate.
What areas do you serve?
We operate nationwide across all 50 states with remote pilots. International projects available upon request.
Can I cancel or change my plan?
Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time with 30-day notice. No long-term contracts.
Are your pilots certified and insured?
All our pilots are FAA Part 107 certified, fully insured, and undergo rigorous safety training. We carry $2M liability insurance.
